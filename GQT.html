Murdeshwar

Murdeshwar, a coastal town in Karnataka, India, is renowned for its majestic 123-foot statue of Lord Shiva—the second tallest in the world. Nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats, it offers stunning ocean views and a peaceful atmosphere.

Mini Taj Mahal

Often called the Mini Taj Mahal, the Bibi Ka Maqbara in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, is a striking replica of the iconic Taj Mahal. Built by Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb’s son in memory of his mother, it showcases beautiful Mughal architecture with serene gardens and white marble.

Ayodhya

Ayodhya, located in Uttar Pradesh, is one of the holiest cities in India and the legendary birthplace of Lord Rama. Rich in history and spiritual significance, it draws millions of devotees to its temples. The recently constructed Ram Mandir has made Ayodhya a major pilgrimage destination.
